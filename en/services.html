SERVICES AND FACILITIES

CAfÉ - BAR - RESTAURANT

Our on-site cafe, bar & restaurant is the perfect place to relax on your holiday. Enjoy freshly ground coffee or a range of delicious teas with home-made cakes, French patisserie and tasty snacks.

More

We have a range of beers brewed locally in Burgundy and our bar would not be complete without a vast Burgundy wine list. Relax on our comfy sofas and enjoy the 'Gout de Bourgogne' - the taste of Burgundy as we present you with wine tasting and local specialities all from our beautiful region of Burgundy. Soft music plays gently in the back-ground as you watch the world drift by.

In the evening, our cafe is transformed into a beautiful, candle-lit, elegant restaurant where you can enjoy local & international cuisine, mingle with the locals and enjoy weekly cocktails whilst soft Jazz plays lightly in the back-ground. Don't think though, that this elegance and sophistication is expensive - our 3-course Menu de Jour (Menu of the Day) starts at only 13 euros per head including delicious local wine and coffee. Of course children are welcome and we offer Children's menus as well as catering for all dietary requirements.

GAMES ROOM

Our games room is open all day and is sure to entertain everyone.

More

Our Games room is open all day and will surely entertain all the family if the weather isn't too great or you want a lazy day on-site. There is always a pool table and table-football. Other games are available depending upon the season.

TENNIS COURTS

Our site offers two full sized tennis courts. These are available to be booked (extra cost) by the hour, or you can book unlimited access by the day, weekend or week.

More

We have a selection of tennis rackets and tennis balls available freely when using the courts. We also organise an annual tennis competition weekend (see our Events page) during which many local people and teams compete for prizes - we provide a free BBQ and drinks over this weekend too.

wc

SHOWER & TOILET FACILITIES

Our sanitary block was completely refurbished in 2016. We had one aim in mind: Luxury. Whilst this IS a campsite, we wanted to provide the kind of facilities you would find in a luxury hotel.

More

This is why you will find individual private cubicles, mirrors, luxury rain-fall showers with massage shower jets, all in your own large, private shower-cubicle with ample changing space.

OUTDOOR PLAY AREA

The Play Zone will keep even the most active kids entertained.

More

There is a huge play area located 200m from the camping and set in a beautiful park adjacent to the river Yonne - there are swings, slides, climbing frames, boules, picnic tables, etc - all with a stunning view of the Saussois Rocks.

ON-SITE SHOP

Our on-site shop is not only the shop for our campers, but for local villagers too.

More

We offer a wide selection of locally produced vegetables, meat, bakery products plus tinned goods and fresh & frozen foods. We pride ourselves on offering locally sourced goods giving you the best that Burgundy has to offer. From our shop, you can also pre-order delicious picnic hampers to take with you on your daily travels or we can make up fantastic hamper-gifts to take home to your friends and family, showcasing all the best of what you've enjoyed in Burgundy.

CAMPER'S KITCHEN

For those guests who are travelling light, our Camper's Kitchen offers the perfect way to cook-up your meals and make snacks or hot drinks.

More

Open all day, our Camper's Kitchen offers free-to-use: Fridge, Freezer, Kettle, Toaster, Micro-wave, Electric Oven & Hot-Plates plus utensils, crockery and cutlery. All we ask is that you clean and tidy-up after using this area.

LAUNDRETTE

We have a small laundrette on-site offering two coin-operated washing machines and two coin-operated tumble-dryers.

More

An iron and ironing board is available free of charge from the reception area.

FREE WIFI & INTERNET

Throughout our cafe, bar, restaurant and games room we offer free WiFi connection plus in the games room is a free-to-use computer with internet access.

More

There are parental locks in place for safe under 18s usage.

WASHING-UP FACILITIES

Don't bother washing up in your unit in a tiny sink, bring all your dirty pots to our washing-up area (or better still, treat yourself and don't bother with the cooking and eat in our restaurant).

More

Large double sinks, draining board and unlimited hot-water for when you've used every plate, pot and pan you've brought with you!

OUR CAMPING

Nestled at the foot of the famous Roches du Saussois rocks and on the banks of the River Yonne, our beautiful campsite is the perfect base to explore all the Burgundy has to offer.
